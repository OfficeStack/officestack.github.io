What do we do?

We jumpstart development on open-source solutions to business software needs.

A lot of the existing solutions carry a lot of baggage: They often have a few years to couple decades of legacy code. They're often bloated and unfriendly.

Our aim is to make software that helps people be more productive and happier employees.

Why do we do it?

Short Answer

Fun. This is how university students unwind these days.

Long Answer

Every office uses software to operate day-to-day, but not everyone can afford SAP, Salesforce, or other such products so they're stuck using something much worse.

We want to provide a open-source counterweight to such products without usage restrictions and licensing fees.

If we can make it work well & look sharp, than doggone it we're sure as hell gonna do it.

What have we done?

We've built and are building some cool things, and are in the process of planning more cool things to build.
